Hello Worlds

Python 3 Physics Application

Using pygame and a homemade physics engine, Hello Worlds is about slinging yourself around the galaxy with limitless creativity.

Linguini

Deep Learning Kitchen Assistant

"Text me when my steak is medium rare." Linguini uses deep learning, OpenCV, a longwave infrared sensor, and a custom ingredient-mapping library to figure out what you can make and then do it right.

Infant Cognition Research

Computerized detection of ADHD

The 30-year-old Visual Expectation Paradigm says that by watching an infants gaze we can detect early signs of ADHD. Using eye tracking in Python, I lead the first team ever to computerize the test, turning the result time from hours to seconds.

Custom Smartlock

3D printed RFID & Arduino lock

Who wants to carry around physical keys? Not me. I designed a smart lock for my apartment door using Fusion360. It uses an Arduino and an RFID reader/writer to scan my school ID card as a key.
